Our Mission

Where Heritage Meets Modern Living

✦

At NF Crafts India, we merge time-honoured artisanal weaving traditions with natural materials and a contemporary design sensibility. Our home décor collections are born not in factories, but in the hands of women who have woven these patterns into the fabric of their culture for centuries.

We believe beautiful objects should do more than adorn a space — they should uplift the people who create them, sustain the communities they come from, and honour the earth that provides every fibre.

Empowering Women

Every Thread, a Step Toward Independence

Behind every basket, every woven mat, every pendant lamp — there is a woman who learned her craft from her mother, her grandmother, her village. NF Crafts India exists to ensure that skill is never wasted.

We provide fair wages, safe working conditions, skill development, and market access these artisans could never reach alone. Our work is not charity. It is commerce with conscience.

◈
Fair & Transparent Wages Every artisan earns above the regional fair wage, paid directly and on time.
◈
Skill & Design Training Monthly workshops in design literacy, quality standards, and craft evolution.
◈
Community & Childcare Support Partnership with local NGOs to provide childcare so mothers can work freely.
The Making

From Earth to Your Home

01
Harvesting

Natural grasses, jute, and bamboo are sustainably harvested by hand — never over-farmed, always regenerative.

02
Preparing

Raw materials are cleaned, dried, and dyed using natural plant-based pigments. No harmful chemicals, ever.

03
Weaving

Skilled women artisans weave each piece entirely by hand using techniques passed through generations.

04
Quality & Care

Every piece is quality-checked, tagged with the artisan's name, and packed with care for its journey to you.
